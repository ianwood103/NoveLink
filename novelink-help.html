What is NoveLink?

NoveLink is an online publishing platform that allows for authors to publish their works to the Internet in a free, yet highly proffessional environment. By keeping many works of literature all in the same place for free, NoveLink also allows for readers to access certain works of their favorite authors at no additional cost.

Since both authors and users can have publicly visible accounts, NoveLink allows for fans to directly communicate with and support their favorite authors along with creating discussions with their peers. Users can also create and join guilds, which act as an online discussion area for certain topics.

Our Mission Statement

NoveLink is a technology platform that seeks to link various peoples around the globe as well as grow the influence and success of small authors through the unifying power of literature. NoveLink does this by incorporating its strong values of keeping literature free and accessible to everyone as well as providing an enjoyable, ad-free experience. NoveLink is fueled by contributors who share a love for literature as well and can see the tremendous value in encouraging its growth.
